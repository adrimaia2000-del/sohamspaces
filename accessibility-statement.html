Policies

Accessibility Statement.

Ancient wisdom should be available to all. We are committed to making this space as inclusive as possible.

Our commitment

SOHAMSPACES is committed to making sohamspaces.com accessible to everyone, including people with disabilities. We believe that ancient wisdom should be available to all, and that starts with making our website as inclusive as possible.

What we are doing

We are continually working to improve the accessibility of our website. Our efforts include:

  • Using clear, simple language throughout our site
  • Ensuring sufficient colour contrast between text and backgrounds
  • Using descriptive text for images where possible
  • Ensuring our site is navigable by keyboard
  • Working within the Wix platform, which follows WCAG 2.1 accessibility guidelines

Known limitations

As a small independent brand, we are aware that our website may not yet meet every accessibility standard. We are committed to improving over time and welcome any feedback that helps us do better.

Alternative formats

If you need any information from our website in an alternative format, please contact us at info@sohamspaces.com and we will do our best to accommodate your request.

Feedback & contact

If you experience any difficulty accessing content on sohamspaces.com, or if you have suggestions for how we can improve, please reach out to us at info@sohamspaces.com. We will do our best to respond within 2 business days and to find a solution that works for you.

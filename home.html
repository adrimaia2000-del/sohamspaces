What is Sohamspaces

A space to pause,
breathe, and return.

Soham is a Sanskrit mantra, "I am that." It is the sound of the breath itself, the oldest teaching there is. Sohamspaces was born from a journey across four countries in search of what it means to live that truth.

What emerged was a collection of teachings, tools, and practices to carry ancient wisdom into an ordinary day.

Our Philosophy

Wisdom is not
acquired. It is
remembered.

The oldest teachings of yoga are not complicated. They were never meant to be. They were designed to be carried, in the body, in the breath, in a single word held with attention throughout a day.

Sohamspaces exists to make that carrying easier. Not to add more to your life, but to help you return to what has always been there.

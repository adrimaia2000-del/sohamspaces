Policies

Shipping Policy.

We ship worldwide. Here is everything you need to know about getting your deck to you.

Where we ship

We ship the SOHAMSPACES deck worldwide, including the United Kingdom, Europe, and internationally. If you are unsure whether we ship to your location, please contact us at info@sohamspaces.com before placing your order.

Processing time

All orders are processed within 2–5 business days of payment confirmation. You will receive a dispatch confirmation email with tracking information once your order has been shipped.

Estimated delivery times

  • United Kingdom — 2–5 business days
  • Europe — 5–10 business days
  • Rest of the world — 10–21 business days

These are estimates only. Delivery times may vary due to postal service delays, customs clearance, or other factors outside our control.

Shipping costs

Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on your delivery address and order weight. We aim to keep shipping costs as fair as possible for all our customers.

Customs & import duties

For international orders, customs fees, import duties, or taxes may be applied by your local authority upon arrival. These charges are the responsibility of the customer. SOHAMSPACES has no control over these fees and cannot predict their amount.

Tracking your order

Where available, a tracking number will be included in your dispatch confirmation email. Please allow 24–48 hours for tracking information to update after dispatch.

Lost or delayed parcels

If your order has not arrived within the estimated timeframe, please contact us at info@sohamspaces.com. We will investigate with the carrier and do everything we can to resolve the issue promptly.

Contact us

For any shipping questions, reach us at info@sohamspaces.com. We aim to respond within 2 business days.
